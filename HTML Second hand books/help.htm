How to buy?

Here are the steps that you should follow!

1. Go to the category page by clicking Category
2. Search your desired books in the search bar or click the quick options below it.
2.1 Or you can choose the books category. (English or Malay)
3. Click on the book that you have searched.
This is your book details.
4. Click buy if you are confirm to buy this book.
4.1 Click Add to cart if you have not sure yet.
This is your book details.
5. Insert the date, time, venue that you want to meet with the seller
6. Choose your payment method.
7. Click continue to proceed.
This is your book details.
8.1 Click download button to download your receipt so you can show it to seller.
8.2 Or you can print the hardcopy for seller.
9. Back to Home page.
